What is Cloud Computing?

In simple terms, cloud computing means storing and accessing your data and applications online (on the internet) instead of on your own computer or a physical server, allowing you to access them from anywhere with an internet connection. Cloud computing allows businesses and individuals to store and access data over the internet instead of on local servers.
